# EnergiseSmart

## Website Link
https://ta32.me/

## Project Description

EnergiseSmart is an innovative platform designed to help Melbourne residents optimize their energy consumption, reduce electricity costs, and make informed decisions about household energy use. Tailored for middle to low-income households, it provides essential tools for managing electricity consumption, planning for energy efficiency, and accessing government support.

What makes EnergiseSmart unique is its interactive design that inspires residents to reduce their electricity bills by making small changes for big savings, all while promoting a sustainable and energy-efficient future.

## Features

1. **AI-Powered Appliance Detection**: Automatically detects and ranks household appliances by energy consumption through image uploads.
2. **Personalized Bill Estimation**: Provides customized electricity bill estimates based on appliance usage, household size, and energy provider.
3. **Government Subsidies and Renewable Energy Programs**: Compiles and offers insights into available subsidies and renewable energy options.
4. **Interactive 3D Home Model**: Users can explore energy-saving tips in an interactive 3D model of their home.
5. **Future Predictions**: Provides data-driven insights into future electricity price trends and emission predictions.
6. **Appliance Comparison**: Enables easy comparison of energy-efficient brands and star-rated models.
7. **Seasonal Trend Analysis**: Delivers insights into seasonal and future consumption trends based on household size.
8. **Carbon Footprint Calculator**: Allows users to gauge their environmental impact based on monthly bills.

## Stack

- **Architecture:** MERN (MySQL, Express.js, React.js, Node.js)
- **Frontend:** React.js, JavaScript, HTML, CSS
- **Frontend Libraries:** GSAP, Three.js, ShaderGradient, Vanta.js, D3.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MySQL
- **Cloud (AWS):** EC2, ECS, API Gateway, RDS
- **Deployment:** GitHub Actions, Docker, Bash
- **Image Detection:** Flask, Python, YOLO v8 (Customized)
